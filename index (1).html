About Me

Who I Am

I'm Abhishek Yadav, a Computer Science & Engineering student at Lovely Professional University with a deep passion for cybersecurity and web development. I blend offensive security skills with full-stack development to build and secure modern web applications.

My security research has earned me recognition in global Hall of Fames — finding critical vulnerabilities like authentication bypasses, CSRF, unrestricted file uploads, and rate-limiting flaws in major platforms including PhonePe, Quizlet, and PDQ.
